Project Background

Nearly 9,500 people die in London every year due to long term exposure to air pollution. For the past 5 years London has been in breach of the EU NO2 safety limit. Because of this we have been tasked to design a smartwatch app that gets up to date data about the levels of air pollution as well as other forms of pollution so people can make more informed decisions about where in the city they go. Our clients are Nicolai Marquardt and Steve Houben
