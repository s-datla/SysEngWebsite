Bi-Weekly Reports

Overview

Here you can access the Bi-weekly Reports made over the course of COMP204P and COMP205P modules.

The reports are separated by Team and cover the following content:

  • An overview of what the group has done over the last two weeks, highlighting progress, successes and any problems.
  • A summary of meetings held, including those with the client.
  • A list of tasks completed, including an estimate of whether the project is running on time.
  • Highlighted Problems that should be resolved before the next meeting.
  • The plan for the next two weeks.
